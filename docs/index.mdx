## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Alert received">
    CauseFlow receives alerts from your monitoring tools via webhooks, or you create an incident manually from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI analysis">
    CauseFlow's AI classifies the incident severity and analyzes the relevant data sources — logs, metrics, infrastructure, code, and databases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Root cause report">
    Findings are synthesized into a root cause explanation with supporting evidence and a confidence score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remediation proposal">
    CauseFlow proposes specific remediation actions — restart a service, rollback a deployment, or create a pull request with a fix.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Human approval">
    Nothing executes without your approval. Review the proposed actions and approve, reject, or request more investigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continuous learning">
    After each resolved incident, CauseFlow extracts patterns from the investigation. These patterns accelerate future analyses and surface known solutions automatically — so every investigation makes the next one faster and more accurate.
  </Step>
</Steps>
