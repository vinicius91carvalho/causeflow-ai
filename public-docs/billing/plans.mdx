CauseFlow offers four plans to match your team's investigation volume. All plans include every integration in the catalog — plans differ in how many investigations and events you use per month and the level of support you receive.

For full pricing details, visit [causeflow.ai/pricing](https://causeflow.ai/pricing).

|------|---------|------------------|---------------------|-------------|------------|

| **Pro** | **$349** | $297 / mo | 60 | 3,000 | 500 req/min |
| **Business** | **$899** | $719 / mo | 200 | 10,000 | 2,000 req/min |
| **Enterprise** | Custom (min $2,000) | Negotiated | Custom | Custom | Custom |

<Note>
  All plans include access to every integration in the catalog. Plans differ in investigation and event volume and support level — not in which tools you can connect.
</Note>

## What counts as an investigation?

One investigation covers a complete incident investigation cycle — from alert ingestion through AI analysis to remediation proposals.

## What counts as an event?

Every incoming alert consumes **1 event**. Events cover AI triage — severity classification and initial evidence gathering. If the alert meets your severity threshold, it also triggers a full investigation.

Each plan includes a monthly allowance of both investigations and events. If you reach your limit, you can purchase more.

## Exceeding your limits

When you reach your monthly investigation or event allowance, you have two options:

- **Overage (auto-charge)** — Additional usage is billed automatically via Stripe at the end of the billing cycle.
- **Quota packs (manual purchase)** — Buy additional investigations or events upfront at a reduced per-unit rate from **Dashboard > Billing > Buy Quota**.

See [Usage and quotas](/billing/usage-and-credits) for details on how consumption works.

## Enterprise

Enterprise plans are available for organizations with 50 or more developers. Enterprise includes:

- Custom investigation and event volumes
- SSO / SAML for your identity provider
- Dedicated support engineer
- Custom SLA
- Custom integrations
- Negotiated overage pricing

[Contact sales](mailto:sales@causeflow.ai) to discuss an Enterprise plan.

## Billing and payments

All payments are processed securely via **Stripe**. You can view invoices, update your payment method, and manage your subscription from **Dashboard > Billing**.

Annual plans offer a **15% discount** compared to monthly billing. See [Manage subscription](/billing/manage-subscription) for upgrade and downgrade instructions.
