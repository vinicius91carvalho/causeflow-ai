The open-source CauseFlow distribution does **not** sell subscriptions or process payments.
Operators self-host the stack; there is no plan upgrade, payment method, or hosted checkout flow to follow.

## What to do instead

1. Clone the [CauseFlow monorepo](https://github.com/causeflow).
2. Run `./init.sh` from the repository root.
3. Open the local dashboard at `http://localhost:3001` and continue with the [Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart).

For full setup instructions, use the [published docs](https://vinicius91carvalho.github.io/causeflow-ai/) and [Open-source usage](/billing/plans).

## Questions

If you need help with a self-hosted deployment, open a discussion or issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/causeflow) or email [support@causeflow.ai](mailto:support@causeflow.ai).
