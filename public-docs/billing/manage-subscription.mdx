title: "Manage subscription"
description: "Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your CauseFlow subscription."
---

All subscription management is handled through **Dashboard > Billing**. Payments are processed securely via Stripe.

## Upgrading your plan

1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade plan**.
3. Select your new plan and click **Continue to checkout**.
4. Complete the Stripe checkout with your payment details.

Plan upgrades take effect **immediately**. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing cycle, and your new investigation and event allowance is applied right away.

<Tip>
  If you are approaching your monthly investigation limit, upgrading mid-cycle is the fastest way to increase capacity without waiting for the next billing period.
</Tip>

## Downgrading your plan

1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**.
2. Click **Change plan** and select a lower-tier plan.
3. Confirm the downgrade.

Plan downgrades take effect at the **end of the current billing period**. You retain your current plan's features and allowance until then. At renewal, your plan switches to the lower tier.

<Note>
  If your current usage exceeds the limits of the downgraded plan, you will be notified before the downgrade is confirmed.
</Note>

## Viewing invoices

1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**.
2. Click **Billing Portal**.

This opens the Stripe customer portal, where you can:

- View and download past invoices
- Update your payment method
- Update your billing address and tax information

## Cancelling your subscription

1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription**.
3. Confirm the cancellation.

Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account is deactivated. Your data, incidents, and investigation history are preserved — reactivate at any time by choosing a new plan.

<Warning>
  Cancelling does not delete your account or data. If you want to delete your account entirely, contact [support@causeflow.ai](mailto:support@causeflow.ai).
</Warning>

## Quota packs

If you need additional investigations or events beyond your plan allowance, you can purchase quota packs upfront at a reduced per-unit rate. Quota packs are consumed before overage charges apply and do not expire within the calendar year.

To purchase quota packs, go to **Dashboard > Billing > Buy Quota** or [contact sales](mailto:sales@causeflow.ai).

## Enterprise plans

Enterprise plan changes — upgrades, custom quota volumes, contract renewals — are managed directly with the CauseFlow sales team. The self-serve billing portal does not apply to Enterprise accounts.

[Contact sales](mailto:sales@causeflow.ai) for Enterprise billing inquiries.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens to unused allowance at the end of the month?">
    Monthly investigation and event allowances reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused allowance does not roll over. Quota packs are the exception — they do not expire within the calendar year.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans more than once in a billing cycle?">
    Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current period — so if you downgrade and then upgrade again, the downgrade is cancelled and only the upgrade applies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Prorated refunds are available within 7 days of an accidental upgrade. Contact [support@causeflow.ai](mailto:support@causeflow.ai) with your invoice number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    CauseFlow accepts all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Annual Enterprise plans can be invoiced with NET-30 payment terms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
