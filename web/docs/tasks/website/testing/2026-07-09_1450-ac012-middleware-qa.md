## Defect fixed

**Spec says "45+ bot user-agent substrings" but implementation had only 20.**

AC-012 states: "Crawler detection: 45+ bot user-agent substrings (googlebot, bingbot, slurp, ...) never get redirected."

`apps/website/src/middleware.ts` defined `CRAWLER_UA_PATTERNS` with exactly 20 entries. The concrete Googlebot test passed, but the coverage breadth (20 vs 45+) did not meet spec.

**Fix:** Expanded `CRAWLER_UA_PATTERNS` from 20 to 58 entries, adding well-known bot/crawler substrings from AI crawlers (gptbot, claudebot, perplexity, anthropic-ai, etc.), monitoring services (pingdom, statuscake, uptimerobot, datadog agent), social/platform link previewers (discordbot, slack, teams, skype, zoom), accessibility tools (voiceover, chromevox, jaws), search engine variants (googlebot-image, googlebot-mobile, bingpreview, exabot), ad crawlers (adsbot, adidxbot), academic scanners (turnitinbot, heritrix), and more. Source: well-known crawler lists collated from commercial crawler blocklists and the repair plan's suggested entries.
